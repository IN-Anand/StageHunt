StageHunt Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your StageHunt account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the StageHunt app and log in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm account deletion.
  4. If you face any issues, contact us at contact.stagehunt@gmail.com.

Data that will be deleted: Your personal information (name, email, phone number), uploaded images/videos, booking history, and chat messages.

Data that may be retained: Some transactional records may be kept for up to 3 years for legal or compliance purposes.

For any further assistance, reach out to us at contact.stagehunt@gmail.com.
