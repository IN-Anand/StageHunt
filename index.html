2. Data we collect

A. You provide

  • Account & profile: name, phone, email, city, categories/act types, pricing, duration, bio, profile photo, intro video.
  • Identity & verification: organization verification details you submit (text, attachments).
  • Content & activity: messages, reviews/ratings, feed posts, likes, comments.
  • Bookings: request details, dates, locations (as provided by users), status and updates.
  • Support: messages you send to support and their metadata.

B. Collected automatically

  • Device & log: device model, OS, app version, language, timestamps, crash logs, diagnostics.
  • Usage: screens viewed, taps, in-app events (e.g., video viewed, booking created).
  • Network: coarse IP-based region for security/fraud and service quality.

C. From others

  • Reports: when users report accounts or content.
  • Third-party sign-in (if enabled): basic profile and auth token.

3. How we use data

  • Provide and improve the App (profiles, search, feed, bookings, messaging, verification).
  • Authenticate users, maintain sessions, and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • Show public profile info (e.g., name, act type, pricing, city, ratings, intro video) to other users.
  • Process bookings and send related communications and alerts.
  • Moderate content, handle reports, and enforce our terms.
  • Analytics, performance, and crash diagnostics.
  • Comply with law and respond to lawful requests.

4. Sharing & processors

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as needed to operate the App or as required by law.

A. When you choose to share

  • Public profile info and content you post (e.g., feed updates, intro video) are visible to other users.
  • Messages are visible to conversation participants.

B. Service providers (processors)

  • Hosting & CDN (e.g., cloud infrastructure to serve images/videos).
  • Analytics & crash reporting (to improve performance and stability).
  • Payment processing (if/when paid features exist).
  • Customer support tools (for helpdesk emails/tickets).

C. Legal & safety

We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, or protect users and the public.

5. Legal bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract: to provide the App and features you request (profiles, bookings, messaging).
  • Legitimate interests: prevent abuse, secure our services, analytics to improve the App. We balance these interests against your rights.
  • Consent: where required (e.g., marketing communications, certain device permissions). You can withdraw at any time.
  • Legal obligation: record-keeping, responding to lawful requests.

6. Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required by law. Typical examples:

  • Account data: kept while your account is active; deleted or anonymized after deletion request.
  • Bookings & transactions: retained for statutory/accounting periods.
  • Logs & diagnostics: retained for a limited period for security and debugging.
